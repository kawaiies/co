CURREX Sports Insoles

CURREX Sports Insoles

No two sports are the same, so we developed insoles for every one. CURREX sports insoles deliver grip, support, and fit for better movement, control, and power. Whether your child is on the soccer field, basketball court, tennis court, or golf course, we have the right insole for their sport.

CLEATPRO - Field Sports

Designed for soccer, football, baseball, softball, and track and field. Features a low-volume fit and lateral support for cleated sports. The CLEATPRO helps athletes maintain stability during quick cuts, sprints, and directional changes.

Best for: Soccer, Football, Baseball, Softball, Track and Field

BASKETBALLPRO - Court Sports

Optimized for the dynamics of basketball - jumping, cutting, and quick lateral movements. Provides targeted cushioning for impact absorption and arch support for stability during play.

Best for: Basketball, Volleyball

ACEPRO - Tennis and Racquet Sports

Built for multidirectional movements on the tennis court. The ACEPRO provides lateral stability and forefoot cushioning for the quick starts and stops required in tennis, pickleball, and racquet sports.

Best for: Tennis, Racquetball, Squash

PICKLEBALLPRO - Pickleball

Tailored specifically for the lateral movements of pickleball. Provides the perfect balance of court feel, cushioning, and arch support for America's fastest growing sport.

Best for: Pickleball

GOLFPRO - Golf

Designed for the rotational swing and hours of walking on the course. The GOLFPRO provides stability through the swing and all-day comfort for walking 18 holes.

Best for: Golf

Dynamic Arch Technology

All CURREX sports insoles feature our patented Dynamic Arch Technology with three interchangeable arch profiles (High, Medium, Low). This allows each athlete to customize their insole to their specific foot type for optimal performance.

Why Sport-Specific Insoles Matter

Every sport places unique demands on your feet. A soccer player needs different support than a basketball player, and a golfer needs different cushioning than a tennis player. CURREX sport-specific insoles are engineered for the specific movements of each sport, helping you perform better and stay injury-free.
